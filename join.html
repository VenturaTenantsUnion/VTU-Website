Join Ventura Tenants Union

We are tenants across Ventura County organized into tenants associations in our buildings and neighborhood-based local chapters. Since 2018, we have stood up to harassment, pressured our landlords to make long-overdue repairs, prevented evictions, protested for better tenant protections. Come to one of our local chapter meetings or any of our events to meet other tenants and join our fight!

Pay Membership Dues

We are funded and run by our members. We do not have staff. Dues money goes to renting meeting spaces, hosting events, and buying supplies like interpretation equipment. We suggest for employed individuals $5/mo, unemployed individuals $1/mo. Any amount is welcome!
